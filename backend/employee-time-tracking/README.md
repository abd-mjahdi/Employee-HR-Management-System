# Employee Time Tracking and Leave Management System

## Project Overview

This is a full-stack web application that helps companies manage employee attendance, track work hours, and handle leave requests in one centralized system. The application replaces manual spreadsheets and email chains with an automated digital workflow that reduces errors and saves administrative time.

## What Problem Does This Solve

Small to medium businesses often struggle with tracking who is working when. Managers need to know employee availability before assigning tasks. Payroll departments need accurate hours to calculate wages. HR needs to track leave balances for compliance. Without a centralized system, this information exists in scattered emails, handwritten notes, and individual spreadsheets, leading to errors, disputes, and wasted time.

This application provides a single source of truth where employees log their hours and request time off, managers approve or deny requests with full context, and HR generates reports for compliance and payroll processing.

## Key Features

The system supports three user roles with different permissions and capabilities.

Employees can log their daily work hours with clock in and clock out times, submit weekly timesheets, request time off by selecting dates and leave types, view their remaining leave balances, see the status of pending requests, and track their work history.

Managers can do everything employees can plus approve or reject time entries from their team members, approve or deny leave requests with visibility into team coverage, view their team's work hours and leave schedules, generate reports about team productivity, and see which team members are currently on leave or will be taking leave soon.

HR Administrators can do everything managers can plus manage all user accounts across the company, configure leave policies like how many vacation days employees receive, adjust leave balances manually when needed, approve leave requests that require HR sign-off in addition to manager approval, generate company-wide reports for payroll and compliance, manage departments and projects, view complete audit trails of all system changes, and configure system-wide settings.
